Welcome to My Page


I am currently enrolled in post-graduate studies in Computer Information Systems at Douglas College, where I am receiving comprehensive training in a diverse array of modern web and mobile technologies. These include widely used tools and platforms such as Visual Studio, MySQL, and industry-standard project management methodologies like Agile. Through this program, I have built a strong foundation in object-oriented programming, enabling me to approach problem-solving with efficiency and adaptability. This combination of theoretical knowledge and practical expertise enhances my confidence in successfully contributing to roles that require technical proficiency, creativity, and a commitment to delivering high-quality results.
